About NSS at GEC Banka

Our Mission

The National Service Scheme (NSS) at Government Engineering College Banka is dedicated to developing the personalities of its students through community service. We aim to instill a deep sense of social responsibility while providing unbiased service to the community

Our Vision

We envision a society where young minds are actively engaged in nation-building through social service, environmental conservation, and community development.
