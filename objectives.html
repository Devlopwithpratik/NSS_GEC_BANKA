What is NSS Team GEC Banka?

NSS Team GEC Banka is the official chapter of the National Service Scheme (NSS), an initiative under the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports, Government of India. Our primary goal is to engage and motivate Government Engineering College Banka students in nation-building activities that contribute to the social welfare of the community. We strive to foster a sense of social responsibility and encourage students to make meaningful contributions towards the development of society.

Our Objectives

Promote Volunteerism

Encourage students to engage in selfless community service.

Develop Leadership Skills

Foster leadership, teamwork, and organizational abilities through social projects.

Social Awareness

Enhance awareness about societal challenges and empower students to address them.

National Integration

Promote unity and understanding among diverse groups.

Motto: Not Me, But You

This motto emphasizes the philosophy that an NSS volunteer prioritizes the welfare of the community over their own personal interests.

Selfless Service

The idea of working for others without expecting anything in return.

Democratic Living

Appreciating the perspectives of others and showing consideration for fellow human beings.

Collective Welfare

The understanding that an individual's well-being is ultimately connected to the welfare of society as a whole.

Our Mission

To motivate students to participate in community service and nation-building by organizing impactful programs focused on health, education, environment, and social welfare. We aim to foster empathy, responsibility, and teamwork.

  • Promote environmental sustainability and cleanliness.
  • Encourage health awareness and blood donation.
  • Support education through mentorship and outreach.

Our Vision

To create a socially responsible student community that actively contributes to the welfare of society and emerges as compassionate leaders, upholding the values of the National Service Scheme.

Community

Serving local communities through meaningful initiatives.

Development

Building leadership and personality through service.

NSS Team GEC Banka

Empowering students through social service and community development.
